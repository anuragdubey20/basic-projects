About Magic Bus

Magic Bus is a non-profit organization that works to provide children from underprivileged communities with access to education, mentorship, and life skills development. The organization's aim is to break the cycle of poverty through innovative programs that empower children to pursue their dreams.

Impact of Magic Bus

Since its inception, Magic Bus has impacted over 350,000 children in India, providing them with the tools and support they need to succeed. Through educational initiatives, mentorship programs, and sports-based activities, the organization has been able to help children stay in school, improve their literacy rates, and gain critical life skills.

Programs Offered by Magic Bus

  • Sports for Development: Using sports as a medium to teach life skills and leadership qualities.
  • Mentorship and Education: Providing mentorship for children to ensure they stay in school and achieve their full potential.
  • Health and Nutrition: Ensuring that children are in good health and aware of how to maintain healthy lifestyles.

How to Get Involved
